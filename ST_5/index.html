Subteam 5 (ST-5) - Reproducibility Review

This subteam collaborates with some statistical journals to review reproducibility materials of submitted manuscripts. As feasible, Editors and Associate Editors of those partnership journals can assign the task of revivewing reproducibility materials (e.g., code) to a member of this subteam. The reproducibility reviewers, please refer to The JASA Reproducibility Reviewer Guidelines for expections when reviewing such materials, and their associated Evaluation Criteria.
