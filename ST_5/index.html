Subteam 4 (ST-4) - Digital Health and Outcomes

This subteam covers innovative and emerging data sources and develops associated study design and analysis methods. Some topics include electronic health records, digital endpoint development , wearable devices, practical guidance of leveraging real-world data, pharmacovigilance, insight generation from real-world evidence and analysis of treatment regimes.
