Mission Statement:

ASA Biopharmaceutical Section (BIOP) Scientific Working Group COMPASS (Computational, Objective-driven, and Meta Platform for Advanced Statistical Science) develops, promotes and educates advanced computational methods for complex multimodal data to ethically, efficiently and precisely deliver medical products to patients.
